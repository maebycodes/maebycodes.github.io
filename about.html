About Me

Hello there, this is Maeby! I’m here on the Internet to articulate what I’m about. I started on the medium of Twitter, where I discovered that I have a lot to say, and that the people who understand what I say are out there. I’m now moving on to longform, both here and on Substack, to create a clearer distillation of my values and beliefs and way of existing, and to become 20% more legible, in order to draw even more of my people (the ones who are not Twitter addicts) to me.

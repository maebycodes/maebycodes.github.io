Dancehole

Dancehole is a guided dance with the energy of an exorcism.

Like an ecstatic dance, it is a space for authentic physical expression—but expression of all valences. Not just airy happy wispy ecstasy: uncomfortable ecstasy, socially unacceptable ecstasy, stupid ecstasy; sorrowful shameful reverent horny despairing euphoric rageful ecstasy. I hold a space that welcomes ugliness and awkwardness and uncertainty, and invites our demons to dance together.

I have run Dancehole at festivals around Europe and the US, and am currently working to bring it to Berlin. Stay tuned for updates on future dances, or reach out below about booking me for a session:
